PPT

Study Room iTracker

The Study Room iTracker is a program used to track the usage of various study rooms in the Folsom Library. Users are able to see the times when others are using the study rooms while the library is open. They also can reserve the rooms for a set amount and can do so by filling out a form on the website. Lastly there are diagrams that direct users to the study room they reserved on the website too.
